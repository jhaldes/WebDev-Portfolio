About Me

I am a freshmen at Lane Tech High School in Chicago. I am part of the Alpha and CS concentrations, and I have a 4.0 GPA. After school I go to robotics in the JV CORE subgroup on Mondays through Thursdays. On the weekends, I 3D print a wide variety of projects, as well as some of the other Alpha student's science fair projects. I know Python and a small amount of C/C++, and I am proficient in Arduino and learning STM32. I aspire to go to college for an engineering field (likely in some form of embedded systems or aerospace).
